Frequently asked questions

What is a sentence generator used for?
Sentence generators are used for creative writing prompts, grammar practice worksheets, ESL and EFL teaching, storytelling exercises, overcoming writer's block, and generating example sentences for vocabulary study.
Can teachers use this?
Absolutely. Teachers use sentence generators to create grammar exercises, writing prompts, and example sentences for vocabulary lessons. The grammar practice mode is especially useful for classroom activities.
Are the sentences random?
Yes — each generation produces a unique combination of subjects, verbs, objects, and modifiers. The same type will always produce different results.
Is it free?
Yes — completely free with no account or signup required.
