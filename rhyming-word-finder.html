Frequently asked questions

Is this rhyme finder free?
Yes - completely free with no account or signup required. Find as many rhymes as you need.
What is the difference between perfect and near rhymes?
Perfect rhymes share the exact same ending sound (cat/hat). Near rhymes (also called slant rhymes) share a similar but not identical sound (cat/back). Both are commonly used in poetry and songwriting.
Can I use it for songwriting?
Absolutely. The syllable filter is especially useful for songwriting, letting you find rhymes that fit specific rhythmic patterns in your lyrics.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes - the rhyme finder is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets.
