About ScrambLab

Free word tools built for students, teachers, writers, and word game lovers everywhere.

Our Mission

ScrambLab was built with a simple idea: word tools should be free, fast, and frustration-free. No accounts, no paywalls, no downloads — just open the tool and start creating. Whether you're a teacher building a classroom activity, a student studying vocabulary, or a word game enthusiast looking for that perfect anagram, ScrambLab has a tool for you.


We believe that learning through word games is one of the most effective and enjoyable ways to build vocabulary and language skills. Every tool on ScrambLab is designed to make that process as easy and fun as possible.

What We Stand For

🆓
Always Free
Every tool on ScrambLab is completely free to use with no account required. We keep the lights on through advertising, not subscriptions.
⚡
Instant Results
All our tools run directly in your browser. No waiting for servers, no loading spinners — results appear the moment you need them.
🔒
Your Privacy Matters
We never store the text you enter into our tools. Everything runs locally in your browser and stays on your device.
📱
Works Everywhere
Every tool is fully responsive and works on phones, tablets, and desktops. Use ScrambLab in the classroom or on the go.
