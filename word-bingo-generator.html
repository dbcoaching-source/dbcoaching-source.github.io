Frequently asked questions

How many words do I need?
You need at least 24 unique words for a standard 5×5 bingo card with a free center space (25 squares minus 1 free space = 24 words). If you have more words, each card will use a random selection, making every card unique.
Can I use this for classroom vocabulary?
Absolutely — word bingo is one of the most engaging ways to practice vocabulary in the classroom. Enter your vocabulary words, generate multiple unique cards so every student gets a different card, and call out definitions instead of the words for an extra challenge.
Is this tool free?
Yes — completely free with no account or signup required.
