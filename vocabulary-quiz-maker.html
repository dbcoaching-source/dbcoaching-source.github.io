Frequently asked questions

Is this vocabulary quiz maker free?
Yes - completely free with no account or signup required. Create as many quizzes as you need.
What quiz formats are available?
Three formats: fill-in-the-blank (students write the word from the definition), matching (draw lines between words and definitions), and multiple choice (four options per question).
How many words can I add?
Up to 20 words per quiz. For the best print layout, 10-15 words works best.
Can I print an answer key?
Yes - click the Answer Key button before printing to show all correct answers on the sheet.
