Frequently asked questions

What is an anagram solver?
An anagram solver takes a set of letters and finds all valid words that can be made using those letters. It's useful for word games like Scrabble, Words with Friends, and Wordle, or for solving word puzzles and jumbles.
How many words are in the dictionary?
ScrambLab's anagram solver uses a dictionary of over 170,000 common English words, including valid Scrabble words from the Official Scrabble Players Dictionary (OSPD).
Can I use it for Scrabble?
Yes — every result shows its Scrabble point value based on standard letter scores. Results are sorted by length and score so you can quickly find your best play.
Does it work on mobile?
Yes, the solver is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets.
