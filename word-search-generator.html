Frequently asked questions

Is this word search generator free?
Yes — completely free with no account or signup required. Generate unlimited puzzles.
Can I use it for my classroom?
Absolutely. Teachers use ScrambLab to create vocabulary worksheets and end-of-lesson activities. The print layout is clean and student-ready.
How many words can I add?
Up to 20 words per puzzle. For the best layout, 8–12 words works best for smaller grids.
Can students solve it on screen?
Yes — turn on Interactive Mode, then click the first and last letter of each word to highlight it. Words are marked off the list as you find them.
