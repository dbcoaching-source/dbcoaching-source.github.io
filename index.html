    Frequently asked questions

    Is this word scramble generator free?
    Yes — completely free, with no account or signup required. Generate as many puzzles as you need.
    Can I use it for my classroom?
    Absolutely. Teachers use ScrambLab to create vocabulary worksheets, spelling practice sheets, and fun end-of-lesson activities. The print layout is clean and student-ready.
    How many words can I add?
    Up to 30 words per puzzle. For the best layout when printing, 8–15 words works best.
    Does it work on mobile?
    Yes. The generator is fully responsive and works on phones and tablets, though printing is easiest from a desktop or laptop.
