Frequently asked questions

Is this crossword maker free?
Yes — completely free with no account or signup required.
How many words can I add?
Up to 20 words. For the best layout with maximum intersections, 8–12 words works best.
Why don't all my words appear in the puzzle?
Words can only be placed if they share at least one letter with another word already in the grid. If a word can't intersect, it may be left out. Try adding more words or words that share common letters.
Can I use it for my classroom?
Absolutely. Teachers use ScrambLab to create vocabulary crosswords, review activities, and end-of-unit puzzles. The print layout is clean and student-ready.
