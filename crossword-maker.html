Frequently asked questions

Is this crossword maker free?
Yes - completely free with no account or signup required.
How many words can I add?
Up to 20 words. For the best layout, 8-12 words works best.
Why do some words not appear?
Words can only be placed if they share at least one letter with another word in the grid.
Can I use it for my classroom?
Absolutely. Teachers use ScrambLab to create vocabulary crosswords and review activities.
