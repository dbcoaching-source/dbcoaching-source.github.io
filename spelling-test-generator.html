Frequently asked questions

Is this spelling test generator free?
Yes — completely free with no account or signup required. Generate and print as many tests as you need.
Can I use it for my classroom?
Absolutely. Teachers use ScrambLab to create weekly spelling tests, vocabulary quizzes, and sight word practice sheets. The print layout is clean and professional.
How many words can I add?
Up to 30 words per test. For a clean single-page print, 10–20 words works best.
Can I print an answer key?
Yes — toggle the Answer Key option before printing to show the correct spelling next to each blank line.
