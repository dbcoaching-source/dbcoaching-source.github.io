Frequently asked questions

What is an acronym?
An acronym is a word formed from the first letters of a phrase. For example, NASA stands for National Aeronautics and Space Administration, and SCUBA stands for Self-Contained Underwater Breathing Apparatus.
What is the difference between an acronym and an abbreviation?
An acronym is pronounced as a word (NASA, SCUBA), while an abbreviation is pronounced letter by letter (FBI, CIA). Both use initial letters, but acronyms form pronounceable words.
Can I use it for business naming?
Yes — the acronym generator is great for creating memorable business names, product names, and organizational names. Type your full name or mission statement to see what acronym it creates.
