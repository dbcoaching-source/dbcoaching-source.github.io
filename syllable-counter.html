Frequently asked questions

Is this syllable counter free?
Yes - completely free with no account or signup required.
How are syllables counted?
Syllables are counted using standard English phonics rules — each vowel sound represents one syllable. Silent vowels and vowel combinations are accounted for. The count may occasionally differ from dictionaries for unusual words.
Why do teachers use syllable counters?
Syllable counting is a fundamental phonics skill. Teachers use syllable counters to help students decode unfamiliar words, improve spelling, and understand word structure. It is also essential for poetry writing and meter analysis.
Can I use it for poetry and haiku?
Absolutely. The syllable counter is perfect for writing haiku (5-7-5), sonnets, and other metered poetry. Use full text mode to check syllable counts across an entire poem.
