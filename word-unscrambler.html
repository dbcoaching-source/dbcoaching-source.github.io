Frequently asked questions

What is a word unscrambler?
A word unscrambler takes a set of jumbled letters and finds all valid English words that can be made from those letters. It is used for word games like Scrabble and Words with Friends, solving word jumble puzzles, and homework help.
How is it different from an anagram solver?
An anagram solver typically finds words that use all your letters. A word unscrambler finds words using any combination of your letters — so you get far more results, including shorter words hidden within your letter set.
How many words are in the dictionary?
ScrambLab's word unscrambler uses a dictionary of over 170,000 common English words, including valid Scrabble words.
