Frequently asked questions

What is word association?
Word association is a cognitive exercise where you respond to a word with the first related word that comes to mind. It is used in psychology, education, creative writing, and language learning to explore connections between concepts and expand vocabulary.
How do I play the word chain game?
Start with any word and keep adding words that are associated with the previous word in the chain. There are no strict rules — any meaningful connection counts. Try to build the longest chain you can without repeating words.
