Frequently asked questions

How are Scrabble points calculated?
Each letter has a point value based on how common it is in English. Common letters like E, A, and I are worth 1 point, while rare letters like Q and Z are worth 10 points. The total word score is the sum of all letter values, before any board multipliers.
Can I use blank tiles?
Yes — type a question mark (?) to represent a blank tile. Blank tiles can stand in for any letter but add 0 points to the word score.
Is this legal to use during a Scrabble game?
That depends on the rules you and your opponents agree on. Many casual games allow word finders, while competitive Scrabble tournaments do not. Always check with your fellow players first.
Is it free?
Yes — completely free with no account or signup required.
