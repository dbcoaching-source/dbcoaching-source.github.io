Frequently asked questions

Is this free?
Yes — completely free with no account or signup required.
How many words are available?
The generator has over 500 words across 10 categories and 3 difficulty levels, ensuring a different experience every time.
Can I use it for my classroom?
Absolutely. Generate a word list, print it, and cut each word into a strip. Students draw a strip and run the hangman game for their classmates without revealing the word.
What difficulty should I choose?
Easy words have 4-5 letters and are great for younger students. Medium words have 6-8 letters and work for most ages. Hard words have 9+ letters and are challenging for adults.
